Tea Gardens' History

Japanese tea is more than just a drink—it's a living tradition, deeply rooted in centuries of culture, spirituality, and craftsmanship.


Tea was first introduced to Japan from China in the early 9th century by Buddhist monks. We've curated only the finest, freshest Japanese teas sourced directly from heritage farms across Japan since 1908.
